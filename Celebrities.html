Hiroyuki Sanade

Hiroyuki Sanada was born the 12th of October 1960 in Tokyo. He originally planned to be an action star, learning Shorinji Kempo style and Kyokushinkai
since he was quite young, building a reputation in the martial arts world, being friends with Jackie Chan and Sonny Chiba, his former master.

He has starred in various films and shows, such as The Last Samurai, Lost, Ring, 47 Ronin... Having the occasion of sharing the stage with acctors like
Tom Cruise or Keanu Reeves.

He has also worked with the Royal Shakespeare Company, participating in several plays like King Lear, for which he was awarded with his entry in the Order of the
British Empire.
